Motihari College Of Engineering, Motihari

        HISTORY

Motihari College of Engineering (MCE), Motihari is a government college, fully funded by the government of Bihar. It is affiliated to Aryabhatta Knowledge University, Patna and approved by AICTE (New Delhi). Previously the institute was known as Indian College of Engineering and was established on 24 November 1980. In 1986, the college was taken over by the Bihar government.

Later in November 2008, the Government reopen the institute & the name was changed from Indian College of Engineering to Motihari College of Engineering. 2018 is the glorious year in the history of college, MCE got 22 faculty members from MHRD-NPIU under TEQIP-III program, and 26 faculties from Bihar Public Service commission in all the departments.

Motihari College of Engineering signed Twinning Agreement with JNTU College of Engineering, Hyderabad at MHRD New Delhi. The agreement was signed to improve quality of teaching-learning at Motihari college of Engineering. The intake of college have increased to 60 in each branch.

        Vision of the Institution

“To facilitate transformation of students into good human beings, responsible citizens and competent professionals for socio economic development of state and nation.”

        Mission of the Institution

1. To inspire socially and economically backward students upon a strong foundation of secular, humanitarian world view.
2. To make the teaching learning process simpler and stronger.
3. To provide quality education to our students.
4. To produce graduates to peruse engineering professions and seek collaboration with institutions of higher learning of repute to upgrade and update the quality of the institution.
5. To create good future citizens for the nation of capability, commitment, conscience and compassion.

        Administration

Administration and Management:- Motihari College of Engineering is a full-fledged Govt. Institution in the Deptt. of Science & Technology, Govt. of Bihar and is bound to function as such. However in Academic and related areas it has to be responsive and participants in the decisions and deliberations of All India Council for Technical Education and other apex bodies like Indian School for Technical Education, Technical Teachers Training Institutes etc. ensuring innovations in Pedagogical and practical aspects of Technical Education. The Administration and Management of the Institute are oriented to these ends too.

Though the Principal is the overall in-charge of all activities and movements of the Institute for smooth functioning and making administration work efficient the following service departments work each under a Professor of proven capability.

        Affiliation

The Institute is academically governed by Aryabhatta Knowlege University, Patna (http://akubihar.ac.in/) which is the degree awarding authority for the institute as B.Tech. and is recognized by the A.I.C.T.E., New Delhi, and the Government of Bihar State.

       Campus and location

Motihari College of Engineering has a permanent campus of 48 acres located at Bairiya Fursatpur, Motihari, East-Champaran district of Bihar in India. It is 8 kilometers from nearest railway station Bapudham Motihari and 5 kilometers from Motihari bus-stand situated at Chhatauni. Regular auto rickshaw services are available from both places to the institute. It is approximately 2.5 kilometers from NH28A.

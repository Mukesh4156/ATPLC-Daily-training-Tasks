Motihari College Of Engineering, Motihari

Motihari college of Engineering (MCE),Motihari is a government college,fully funded by the government of Bihar.
It is affiliated to Aryabhatta Knowledge University, Patna and approved by AICTE(New Delhi).
Previously the institue was known as Indian College of Engineering and was established on 24 November 1980.
In 1986, the College was taken over by the bihar government.
